How Much Can We Move?

Our moving van is specially designed for furniture transport and city moves, offering the largest interior volume in the 3.5t class. Compared to 7.5t trucks, it's easier to park in the city and navigate narrow streets.

In one trip, we can typically move the contents of a 2-room apartment. For distances under 50 km, we can make up to 5 trips in one day, allowing us to move apartments up to 120m² in a single day.

Cargo Space Dimensions:

Length 4.05 m
Height 2.2 m
Width 2 m
Interior volume approx. 18m³

On Moving Day

1.

We protect the floor and entrance door with protective materials.

2.

3.

In the van, the cargo is secured with soft materials and straps.

4.

After transport, we bring everything into the new home and unpack it.
